Moon Jump

How to perform the Moon Jump glitch


1. The first thing you will need to do to perform this glitch is go to the mounted archery camp in Faron.

2. Once your there talk to Jini whilst riding a horse.
3. Instead of following the intend route for the archer turn left and head for some mountains.

4. Once you are on top of the mountains you need to wait till there are fifteen seconds left on the mounted archer timer.
5. Then you will need to jump off your horse and off the mountain to and paraglide on top of the horses in the valley. It is crucial that in all of these steps you cannot touch the ground.
6. Once you have landed on the other random horse stay on it till the archery timer runs out.

7. You will be put into a cut scene that should hopefully show link hopping around and floating on the horse you landed on at the end of the timer.
