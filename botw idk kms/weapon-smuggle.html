Weapon smuggling


Weapon smuggling is a very useful glitch for completing the blue flame quests such as the ones in Akkala and Hatano. This is a very easy to perform glitch that only requires you to have the bomb rune, a weapon which you're going to smuggle and a shield.
1. The first step to performing this glitch is to equip any of the bomb runes but the square bomb works better as it doesn't roll away.
2. Place down the bomb
3. Pull out your weapon by pressing y.
4. Press B to put away your weapon and use your left D pad to unequip your shield.
5. Pick up your bomb then throw with R button.
