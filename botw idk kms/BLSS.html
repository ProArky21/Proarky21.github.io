BLSS

Bow Lift Smuggle Slide


1. The first thing you need to do is find a ledge that allows link to perform a "step up" animation.

2. The second thing that you need to do is be near an item that you can pick up and hold like a rock or bomb.
3. Hold out your shield with Zl then press ZR to pull out your bow then pick up the item you chose that you could pick up with A.
4. Hold out your shield with ZL again and in quick succession press x, B and the plus button.
5. Then you must remove your shield.
6. Hold Xr to load an arrow into your bow then press B to cancel the bow.
7. Find a ledge and go up to it holding B.
8. To stop falling you stop holding B.
