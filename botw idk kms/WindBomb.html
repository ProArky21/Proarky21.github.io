Wind bomb

How to do a Wind Bomb


1. To perform a wind bomb you must find a ledge high enough to go into bullet time. For example, like a tower.
2. then equip the circle bomb rune.
3. Hold ZL to pull out your shield.
4. Walk to the edge of the ledge and you will find that your shield is preventing you from walking off.
5. Press X whilst moving forward then place down you bomb with L immediately after pressing x. Then pull out your bow after travelling the full length of the shield hop. This will put you into bullet time.
6. Switch your runes to the square rune and place it down with the L button.
7. Then switch back runes to the circle bomb rune and explode it. If done correctly you should gain significant directional momentum
